This is a full-stack food and grocery ordering app built with Next.js. It features real-time product browsing, cart management, and a streamlined checkout experience. The app is optimized for performance and mobile-first users, making it easy to order essentials on the go.

## Features

- **Product Listings**: Browse food and grocery items in categorized views.
- **Search & Filter**: Quickly find products with intuitive search and filtering options.
- **Shopping Cart**: Add multiple items to the cart and adjust quantities.
- **Order Checkout**: Seamless checkout flow with address and payment integration.
- **User Authentication**: Sign up, log in, and manage personal profiles.
- **Order Tracking**: Track the status of orders in real-time.

## Technologies

- **Next.js**: For building fast, scalable web applications.
- **Tailwind CSS**: For responsive, utility-first styling.
- **Prisma**: As an ORM to handle database operations.
- **PostgreSQL**: Relational database for managing product and order data.
- **Vercel**: Deployment platform for performance and scalability.
